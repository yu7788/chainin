PCS Project Brief

PCS public chain is a decentralized block chain application development platform supporting multiple industries. Bring the advantages of block chain technology to users of different industries and ordinary Internet users in a more flexible and convenient way.

PCS takes advantage of technological advantages such as commonality and compatibility innovation, improvement of scalability of the underlying public chain, and support of second processing speed

  • Adopting the consensual mechanism of Delegated Proof of Stake
  • avoid the waste of resources in Proof of Power
  • and the centralization trend of the Proof of rights and interests mechanism
  • the system is also faster and more secure, laying the foundation for enterprise application

Seven layer models:
Resource layer、Protocol layer、Consensus layer、Contract layer、Tool layer、Decentralized application layer、Ecological layer
